Introducing PUNY:

The Perl/Unix Integration Society of NY

www.thinman.com/puny
  • We are a special interest group of the NY Software Industry Association dedicated to bringing the value of perl to the people of New York
  • We will be teaching basic perl while demonstrating its usefulness through technical presentations
  • PUNY members will have an unparalleled opportunity to network with the New Media corporations of Silicon Ally as well as many other influential members of the business and educational communities
  • As a member you will be able to participate in system integration projects touching on many other computer languages and operating systems
  • You will also be invited to seminars and workshops discussing a wide variety of systems and new media topics including venture capital, patents, and the "new evangelism"
  • We are meeting under the aegis of the NY Software Industry Association, and will be working closely with the NYPC C++SIG and the Perl Mongers of NY

Please Come to Our First Meeting: Tues, Feb. 22nd, 6:00pm @IBM, 57th and Madison Ave. Room 950

PUNY membership is free and all are invited. The seminars require a $20 donation to the New York Software Industry Association, which supports, among things, student internships and computer education projects in the public school system.Refreshments will be served.Students are free, but please contact john@thinman.com first.
